IDUKKI

Idukki, located in the state of Kerala, India, is a mesmerizing destination known for its breathtaking natural beauty and diverse landscapes. Nestled in the Western Ghats, Idukki is renowned for its lush green hills, scenic valleys, and pristine rivers. It is a paradise for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts alike.
